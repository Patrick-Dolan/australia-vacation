Travel Arrangements

There are a host of ways to reach australia some are much easier and more adviseable than others.

Canoeing

The canoe is one of the oldest methods of travel and you can probably do it if you row real hard and believe in yourself.

  • Cost: $1000
  • Available: Always.
  • Included: Free Rowing practice/exercise.

Water Running

This is probably one of the hardest ways since you'll have to run for many weeks straight. Also you'll have to run fast enough to skim across the water. An added benefit is that if you're the first one to do it you get free citizenship and all the gatorade you can drink.

  • Cost: FREE!
  • Available: Always available but be sure to get good shoes.
  • Included: Cardio, heavy breathing, sweating profusely.

Airplane

While this is the least imaginative way of reaching Australia I hear that it is the most reliable and easy to do. You do have sit on a plane for about 18 hours but in comparison to running that kind of sounds nice.

  • Cost: $2500
  • Available: Always available but you will have to quarantine for 2 weeks when you get there.
  • Included: Free peanuts. (Only available if riding first class or business)

Cruise Ship

Probably the most comfortable way to make your way across the sea.

  • Cost: $1000
  • Available: Ships set out every 3 weeks from available harbours.
  • Included: Movie theatres, old people games, casinos and 3 buffets.
